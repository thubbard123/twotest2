CAN-Bus Cable

To meet ISO 11898, a bus cable with a characteristic impedance of 120 Ω should be used. The maximum usable cable length for reliable communication decreases with increasing transmission speed. As a guide, you can use the following values which has measured; however, these values are not assured limits:

  • Characteristic impedance: 100–120 Ω
  • Cable capacitance max.: 60 nF/km
  • Lead loop resistance: 159.8 Ω/km
