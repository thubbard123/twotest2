I/O Connection

Technical Data Inputs/Outputs -C

Interface

Electrical Data

Digital inputs (X15B)
  • as per IEC61131-2 type 1
  • ON: 15 VDC to 30 VDC, 2 mA to 15 mA
  • OFF: -3 VDC to 5 VDC, < 1 mA
  • update rate 250 µs
  • galvanic isolation for 250 VDC

Digital outputs (X15B)
  • as per IEC61131-2 type 1
  • max. 30 VDC, 100 mA
  • short circuit proof
  • galvanic isolation for 250 VDC

Relay output (X15A)
  • max. 30 VDC, 1A
  • max. 42 VAC, 1 A
  • time open/close 10ms
  • isolation 400 VDC contact/coil

STO-Enable inputs (X16A/B)
  • ON: 18 VDC to 30 VDC, -C with one AKD-N 80 mA, every additional AKD-N + 25 mA
  • OFF: 0 VDC to 12 VDC, < 50 mA
  • immunity 60 VDC

STO-Status outputs (X15A)
  • as per IEC61131-2 type 1
  • max. 30 VDC, 100 mA
  • galvanic isolation for 250 VDC

Technical Data Inputs/Outputs -N

Interface

Electrical Data

Digital inputs (X24)
  • as per IEC61131-2 type 1
  • ON: 15 VDC to 30 VDC, 2 mA to 15 mA
  • OFF: -3 VDC to 5 VDC, < 1 mA
  • galvanic isolation for 30 VDC
  • 2 high speed inputs: update rate 2 µs
  • 1 standard input: update rate 250 µs

Digital output (X24)
  • as per IEC61131-2 type 1
  • max. 30 VDC, 100 mA
  • short circuit proof
  • galvanic isolation for 30 VDC
  • Update rate 250 µs

STO input (X26)
  • ON: 18 VDC to 30 VDC, 80 mA
  • OFF: 0 VDC to 12 VDC, < 50 mA
  • immunity 60 VDC

STO outputs (X24)
  • as per IEC61131-2 type 1
  • max. 30 VDC, 100 mA
  • short circuit proof
  • galvanic isolation for 30 VDC
  • Update rate 62.5 µs
