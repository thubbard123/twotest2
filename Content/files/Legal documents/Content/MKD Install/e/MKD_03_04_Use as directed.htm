Use as Directed

The -C series power supplies are exclusively intended for operating -N servo drive modules within a cabinet and AKD-N decentralized servo drives.

The -N family of servo drives is exclusively intended for driving suitable synchronous servomotors with closed-loop control of torque, speed, and/or position.

system modules are components that are built into electrical plants or machines and can only be operated as integral components of these plants or machines.
