Guide to Mechanical Installation

The following tools are required (at a minimum) to install the -C and -N modules; your specific installation may require additional tools.

  • M4 hexagon socket-cap screws (ISO 4762)
  • 3 mm T-handle Allen key
  • No. 2 Phillips head screwdriver
  • Small slotted screwdriver

For mechanical installation of AKD-N to the machine, refer to the AKD-N Installation Manual.

Install the modules as follows:

  • Prepare the site.
    The modules must be mounted in a closed control cabinet (➜ # 1). The site must be free from conductive or corrosive materials. For the mounting position in the cabinet (➜ # 1).
  • Check ventilation.
    Check that the ventilation of the modules is unimpeded, and keep within the permitted ambient temperature (➜ # 1). Keep the required space clearance above and below the modules (➜ # 1).
  • Check cooling system.
    If cooling systems are used for the control cabinet, position the cooling system so that condensation water cannot drip onto the modules or peripheral devices.
  • Mount the modules.
    Assemble the power supply module and the drive modules on the conductive, grounded mounting plate in the cabinet.
  • Ground the modules.
    For EMC-compliant shielding and grounding, (➜ # 1). Ground the mounting plate, the motor housing, the GND of the 24 V supply and CNC-GND of the control system.
