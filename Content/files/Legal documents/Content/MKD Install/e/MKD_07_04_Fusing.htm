Fusing

External power supply and 24 V fusing

Non renewable cartridge fuses must be used with finger safe fuse holders according to IEC 60529.

UL/cUL cartridge fuses: Class J, 600 VAC 200 kA, time-delay. The fuse must be UL and cUL listed. The voltage rating of the external fuse(s) shall be at least equal to the mains voltage.

CE cartridge fuses: types gS(gRL) or gG, 400 V/500 V, time-delay
