Local STO on AKD-N

General information

The STO safety implementation on the AKD-N is certified. The safety circuit implementation used for the safety function "Safe Torque Off" in the drive is suited for SIL 2 according to IEC 62061 and PLd / CAT3 according to ISO 13849-1.

In case of using an AKD-N with option "DS" or "DT" (local STO input), the global STO signal will have not influence to this specific drive. The local STO input on the AKD-N and -N have no influence to the global STO as well. The local AKD-N STO functionality is described in the AKD-N installation manual.

Refer to the AKD-N Installation Manual for full information.
