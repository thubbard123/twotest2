Mains choke for MKD-C-NA

High Voltage up to 480 V!

Risk of electric shock.

  • Make sure that the cabinet is safely disconnected (for instance, with a lock-out and warning signs).
  • Wait at least minutes after disconnecting the drive from the main supply power before touching potentially live sections of the equipment (e.g. contacts) or undoing any connections.

Due to the high earth leakage currents induced by the system, you should observe the requirements of EN 61800-5-1 (e.g. fixed installation, ≥ 10 mm² or double protective earth) when carrying out mounting and installation work.
Use a shielded cable between choke and MKD-C.

Mounting: 50 mm free space required above and below the device.
Connection diagram: see (➜ # 1)
