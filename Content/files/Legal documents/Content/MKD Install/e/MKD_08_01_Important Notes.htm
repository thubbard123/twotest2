Important Notes

-C and -N devices must be mounted in cabinets only with protection class IP 54 according to IEC 60529.

Protect the device from impermissible stresses. In particular, do not let any components become bent or any insulation distances altered during transport and handling. Avoid contact with electronic components and contacts.

The device will switch itself off in case of overheating. Ensure that the mounting space matches the requirements "Ambient Conditions, Ventilation, and Mounting Position" (➜ p. 1).

Do not mount devices that produce magnetic fields directly beside the device. Strong magnetic fields can directly affect internal components. Install devices which produce magnetic field with distance to the -C and/or shield the magnetic fields.
