Regen resistor connector X3

For technical data on the regenerative brake circuit (➜ # 1).

Fusing (such as fusible cut-outs or power switches) to be provided by the user.

  • optional T version
  • Mating connector data (➜ # 1).
  • X3/5 to X3/6 must be shorted to use the built in regen resistor. Remove the jumper to connect an external regen resistor .
