Motor single cable connection

Motors with two wire feedback systems like SFD3 or Hiperface DSL can be connected to by using a single hybrid cable. Depending on the version (single or dual axis), one or two single cable motor connections are possible.

Motor Power, Brake and Feedback connectors X1, X2

  • X1 (Feedback 1) / X2 (Feedback 2)
  • Motor Power: 4 pin, pitch 7.62 mm
  • Motor Brake: 2 pin, pitch 3.81 mm
  • Motor Feedback: 2 pin, pitch 3.81 mm
  • Cable length: (➜ # 1)
  • Use cables
  • Mating connector data (➜ # 1).
  • Feedback types: SFD3, HIPERFACE DSL
  • Rated voltage 11 V.
  • Rated current is 350 mA simultaneously for X1 and X2.

Pinout is identical for connectors X1 and X2.
