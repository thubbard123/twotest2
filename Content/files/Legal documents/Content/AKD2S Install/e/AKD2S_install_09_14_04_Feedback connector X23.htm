Feedback Connector X23

  • with Safety Option 1: Connectivity Option F3 or DX (➜ # 1)
  • with Safety Option 2 or 3: Connectivity Option F3 (➜ # 1)
  • Sub-D high density 15 pin, female
  • Use feedback cables
  • Input for several feedback types
  • Input for Electronic Gearing, (➜ # 1)
  • Output for encoder emulation (EEO1), (➜ # 1)
  • Digital input (➜ # 1), Digital output (➜ # 1)
  • Mating connector data (➜ # 1).

Encoder power supply (X23 pins 10/11):

  • Maximum voltage 9 V with shorted sense contacts (4/5), rated voltage 5 V +/-5%.
  • Rated supply current is 350 mA.
  • Voltage rise time ~4 ms with full load and 220 µF of capacitance.
  • Encoder power lines capacitance 10 µF to 220 µF
