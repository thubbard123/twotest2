AXIS#.SAFEPARAM.SS1_#.TIMETOSTO

Description

Indicates the maximum time delay in milliseconds to initiate Safe Torque Off (STO) when SS1 is activated.

 

Context

For complete SS1 information refer to SS1-t (Safe Stop 1)SS1-t/r (Safe Stop 1).
