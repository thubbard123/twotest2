AXIS#.SAFEPARAM.SPEEDCOMPUTATIONINTERVAL

Description

Indicates the time interval used to compute the speed in the SMM. This is only applicable when a safe encoder has been configured in the safety parameters.

 

Context

For safe encoder details refer to Motor feedback requirements .
