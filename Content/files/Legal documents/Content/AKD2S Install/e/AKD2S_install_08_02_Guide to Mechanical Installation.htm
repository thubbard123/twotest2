Guide to Mechanical Installation

The following tools are required (at a minimum) to install the ; your specific installation may require additional tools:

  • M5 hexagon socket-cap screws (ISO 4762)
  • 4 mm hexagon ball head screwdriver
  • No. 2 Phillips head screwdriver
  • Small slotted screwdriver

Install the drive unit as follows:

  • Prepare the site.
    Mount the drive in a closed control cabinet (ambient conditions (➜ # 1)). The site must be free from conductive or corrosive materials. For the mounting position in the cabinet (➜ # 1).
  • Check ventilation.
    Check that the ventilation of the drive is unimpeded, and keep within the permitted ambient temperature (➜ # 1). Keep the required space clearance above and below the drive (➜ # 1).
  • Check the cooling system.
    If cooling systems are used for the control cabinet, position the cooling system so that condensation water cannot drip onto the drive or peripheral devices.
  • Mount the drive.
    Assemble the drive and power supply near each other on the conductive, grounded mounting plate in the cabinet.
  • Ground the drive.
    For EMC-compliant shielding and grounding, (➜ # 1). Ground the mounting plate, motor housing and CNC-GND of the control system.
